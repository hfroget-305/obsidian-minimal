Customer journey
Four stages, one flywheel, one improvement loop
🔁 promoters refer the next lead

1 · Attract

  • Channels: web, ads, events, outreach, referrals
  • Lead file created, status: new
  • First response within 24h

2 · Convert

  • Contact → qualify → proposal
  • Every step logged as a touchpoint
  • next_action_date — no proposal left hanging

3 · Deliver

  • Won → customer file, history kept
  • Onboarding: first value fast
  • QBRs, check-ins; health kept fresh

4 · Delight & Refer

  • NPS after milestones
  • 9–10 → ask for the referral, reward it
  • 0–8 → at-risk, win-back play
🔄 Improvement loop Lost reasons and detractor root causes feed a 20-minute weekly review → one small fix ships every week: a template, a playbook, a channel.
